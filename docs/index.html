Privacy Policy for MQ Pay

Last Updated: November 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MQ Pay ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Payment Records: USSD codes, payment amounts, and transaction notes that you manually enter or scan via QR codes
  • Contact Information: Contact names and phone numbers when you choose to link contacts to payment records
  • Backup Files: Data exported for backup purposes when you use the backup/restore feature

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Location Data: Approximate location data may be collected if you grant location permissions (used for location-based services)
  • Device Information: Basic device information necessary for app functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate QR code scanning for payment information
  • To store and manage your payment records locally on your device
  • To enable contact integration for easy payment recipient selection
  • To provide backup and restore functionality for your payment records
  • To enable direct calling and USSD dialing features
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your payment records and app data are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Local data encryption using SharedPreferences
  • Secure file storage for backup files
  • No cloud storage or external data transmission

5. Permissions We Request

MQ Pay requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera: To scan QR codes containing payment information
  • Contacts: To read and select contacts for payment records (optional)
  • Location: To provide location-based services (optional)
  • Phone/Call: To initiate USSD codes and phone calls for payments
  • Storage: To save and restore backup files
  • Internet Access: To check network connectivity status

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains on your device. The only exceptions are:

  • When you explicitly export backup files to share or transfer
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

7. Third-Party Services

MQ Pay uses the following third-party services:

  • Flutter Framework: For app development (Google)
  • Mobile Scanner Plugin: For QR code scanning functionality

These services may have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data at any time through the app settings
  • Export your data using the backup feature
  • Revoke app permissions through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

10. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the app. We do not have access to your data stored on your device.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

12. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers involved in the use of MQ Pay.
